# World and African Chess History

Chess is one of humanity's oldest strategy games. Born in India as *chaturanga*, it spread through Persia and the Arab world before reaching Europe and Africa.

## Africa on the global map

From Egypt to Ethiopia, from Morocco to South Africa, chess has grown through schools, clubs and national federations. African players now compete in Olympiads, Grand Swiss events and online platforms like AFRICHESS.

## Why this matters for you

Understanding where the game comes from helps you appreciate its culture: patience, calculation, respect for the opponent, and the joy of learning.
